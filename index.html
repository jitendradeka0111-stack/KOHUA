KOHUA is a community-led NGO from Assam, working across Northeast India for responsible tourism, conservation, art, music, and the living traditions of indigenous peoples. OUR STORY Born in Assam, belonging to the Northeast KOHUA was born in Assam out of a deep love for Northeast India — its people, its forests, its songs, and its stories. We saw how fast-changing tourism and modernisation were reshaping indigenous communities, and we chose to act.
